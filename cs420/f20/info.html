Textbook

The following textbook is required for this course:

Michael Sipser. Introduction to the Theory of Computation, 3rd edition.

It is widely available, including at the UMB bookstore.

Homework

Homework will be assigned every week.

Homework out: Wednesday

Homework due: Tuesdays 11:59pm

In addition to your solution files, each homework submission must include the following (each individual assignment may have more details):

  • a Makefile with:

    • a setup target that installs the programs needed to run your solutions, and

    • individual targets that run each problem solution.

  • a README file with the following information:

    • Time spent (in hours) on the assignment.

      This will help me gauge whether assignments are too easy or hard.

    • The names of other students you worked with.

      You may discuss homework with other students but anything you submit must be work that you came up with independently. See Academic Honesty.

    • Any other books or websites you used.

      You may of course consult other reference sources while learning the materials.

      But again, any work you submit must be your own.

Grading

Grades will be computed as follows:

  • Homework: 80%

  • Class Participation: 10% (includes lectures, presentations, and discussion board)

  • Quizzes: 10%
