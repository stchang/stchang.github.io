    4 Textbook

    The following textbook is required for this course:

    Michael Sipser. Introduction to the Theory of Computation, 3rd edition.

    It is widely available, including at the UMB bookstore.

    The following textbooks are optional, but may be useful to consult occasionally:

    • Hopcroft, Motwani, Ullman. Introduction to Automata Theory, Languages, and Computation (link to archive.org), 3rd edition. 2007, Pearson

    • Simovici and Tenney. Theory of Formal Languages with Applications. 1999, World Scientific.
